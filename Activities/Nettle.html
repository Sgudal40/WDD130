✨ Nettle the Fairy Druid ✨

🌿 About Nettle 🌿

Nettle is a small, two-foot-tall fairy with dragonfly wings and a sapling growing on his back. Born in the mystical settlement of Sonace, he has always had a deep connection with nature. As a botanist and herbalist, Nettle has honed his craft under a wise and energetic master.

🍃 Abilities & Skills 🍃

  • 🌿 Expert in botany and herbalism
  • 🍄 Skilled druid, harnessing the power of nature
  • 🌱 Can communicate with plants and creatures of the wild
  • 🦋 Stealthy and nimble, perfect for gathering rare herbs

📜 Backstory 📜

Nettle grew up in Sonace, a town surrounded by the dark and magical Maspa forest. Though never treated cruelly, he always felt like an outsider. His love for plants and potions kept him engaged, especially through his friendship with Flotti, a Kenku whose master was a potion brewer. A tragic event severed his last ties to Sonace, leading him to venture into the world in search of purpose.
