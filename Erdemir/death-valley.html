Welcome to Death Valley

Death Valley is a land carved by war and silence. Once open plains, its fields are now churned with mud, bone, and rusted steel. Endless fields stretch between two powers locked in a battle that neither can win: one, upheld by unwavering divine might; the other, driven by the certainty that death is never the end.

While the faithful hold fast to sacred ground and the dead rise again and again, the war simply resets - tireless and unrelenting. The battlefield is quiet only for the brief moments between slaughter, and even then, the silence feels like the breath before a scream.

No one comes here by choice
