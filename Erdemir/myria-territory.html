Welcome to Myria Territory

Myria’s Territory is a land of two realms, one above the waves and one beneath them. On the surface, a proud kingdom of landfolk thrives on a peninsula, its castle town standing as a beacon of seafaring trade and diplomacy. Below, an ancient underwater civilization of merfolk and aquatic beings flourishes in a shimmering domain reminiscent of a lost Atlantis, with towering coral spires and bioluminescent halls.
Bound by an unshakable alliance, these twin kingdoms have long endured where others might have fallen. Trade flows freely between them, their ships and currents carrying goods, culture, and magic between land and sea. Though vastly different in nature, both societies are deeply intertwined, their fates linked by the tides that shape their world.
Yet, Myria’s strength is not without its challenges.
The ocean holds secrets older than the kingdoms themselves, and the unity between land and sea is not always absolute. Pirates, deep-sea horrors, and unseen tensions threaten to disrupt the balance, making Myria a place of both opportunity and peril for those who seek adventure, power, or fortune.
