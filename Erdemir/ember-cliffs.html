Welcome to Ember Cliffs

Ember Cliffs is a land of smoldering peaks and glowing crags, where volcanic heat shapes both the land and the lives of those who call it home. Though the mountains rumble and rivers of lava carve their way through the stone, the region has known relative peace for generations, with eruptions more predictable than feared.

At its heart rises Candlespire, a towering volcano whose steady glow serves as both a beacon and a warning. The cliffs and valleys surrounding it are rich with rare materials, drawing in those resilient enough to harvest the land’s bounty. Settlements cling to the mountainsides, their people accustomed to the heat and the ever-present scent of ash.

Life here is shaped by fire and stone, a test of endurance for those who seek its hidden treasures.
