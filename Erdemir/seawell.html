Welcome to Seawell

Seawell is a bustling hub of commerce where ships from all corners of the world dock, bringing goods from distant lands. Though the actual trading happens elsewhere, the flow of goods through this tropical region keeps it thriving. The villages scattered across Seawell are united by their reliance on trade, but each has its own distinct charm and tight-knit community.

Life in Seawell is vibrant, with locals working as guides and bodyguards for visiting merchants, offering protection and insight as they navigate the sometimes dangerous, sometimes lucrative trade routes. While the rich opportunities in Seawell keep its people busy, there’s no shortage of time for celebration and enjoyment—the air is always full of laughter, music, and stories.

Whether you’re here to trade, explore, or simply take in the sights, Seawell is a place where opportunity and adventure await those with a keen eye and a sense of adventure.
