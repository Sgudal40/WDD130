Welcome to New Varon

New Varon is a land where time holds little meaning, a kingdom where the past lingers, and the future is all but certain. The cities stand in eerie, ageless grandeur, built not for the fleeting concerns of the living but for an eternity of existence. Its rulers, its workers, its scholars—many have walked these streets for centuries, unfazed by the weight of years.
Travelers who enter quickly learn that New Varon is not hostile to the living, but neither does it cater to them. Laws are written with the assumption that permanence is inevitable. Bureaucrats and merchants deal in contracts that extend beyond a single lifetime. Temples and scholars speak not of what comes after death, but of how best to prepare for it. There is always another form, another purpose, another role waiting to be filled.
For those who linger too long, the question is not whether they will change—but whether they will be the ones to decide how.
