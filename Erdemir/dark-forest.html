Welcome to The Dark Forest

The Dark Forest is a realm untouched by time, where towering trees block out the sun and the air hums with ancient magic. Warded against the harsh climates beyond, the forest thrives as a pocket of deep, untamed nature. Dense canopies, glowing fungi, and twisting roots mark a landscape shaped not by seasons, but by the will of the forest itself.

This forest serves as a sanctuary for nature and magic alike, nurturing both in secret and in abundance. The land itself seems to resist intrusion, and those who enter with ill intent often vanish without a trace. A handful of druid circles live in harmony with the forest, though even they speak carefully of what lies deeper in the woods.

Enter only with respect. The forest watches.
