Welcome to Snowpoint Highlands

Snowpoint Highlands stands like many frozen titans, their jagged cliffs and wind-carved valleys wrapped in a blanket of eternal frost. The air is thin, the cold biting, and the silence deep—broken only by the distant call of beasts strong enough to survive here.

Life in the Highlands is harsh but not joyless. While the earth offers little beyond stone and frost, the land provides in other ways. Traders deal in the spoils of great hunts: thick pelts, rare meats, and bones from creatures built for the cold. Communities are tight-knit, fires are always burning, and the meals—rich, hearty, unforgettable—warm the spirit as much as the body.

It’s a realm for the resilient. A place where survival is a craft, and every shared dish is a story told by firelight.
